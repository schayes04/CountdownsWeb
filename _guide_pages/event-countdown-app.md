## A better way to keep dates visible

Calendars are good at storing dates, but they are not always good at making a date feel close. Countdowns turns an event into a visible timer that can sit on your Home Screen, Lock Screen, Apple Watch, iPad, or Mac.

That makes it useful for serious deadlines and fun events alike. One app can handle a work launch, a trip, a birthday, a race, a graduation, or a personal goal.

## Match the countdown to the event

Different events need different displays. A vacation might need weeks and days. A game might need hours. A launch might need exact minutes. Countdowns lets each timer use the units and style that make sense.
