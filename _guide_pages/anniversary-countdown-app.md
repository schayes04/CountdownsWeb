## Useful for recurring and personal milestones

Anniversaries are easy to care about and easy to miss until they are close. A recurring countdown keeps the date visible ahead of time and can reset for the next occurrence after the current one passes.

Use Countdowns for wedding anniversaries, relationship milestones, work anniversaries, sobriety dates, move-in dates, and other personal moments that matter.

## Plan before the reminder is urgent

Set notifications early enough for the way you celebrate. A dinner reservation may need a week. A gift may need longer. A simple message may only need a day. Each countdown can use the notification timing that fits.
