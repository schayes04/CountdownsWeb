## Useful when the countdown is near

Lock Screen widgets are especially helpful when a date is close enough that you want to check it often. A trip, game, birthday, event, or deadline can stay visible without opening Countdowns or scanning your calendar.

Use the app to create and customize the countdown, then pick the widget that fits your Lock Screen layout.

## Pair Lock Screen and Home Screen widgets

Some countdowns belong on both screens. Put a near-term date on the Lock Screen for quick checks, and keep longer-term countdowns or lists on the Home Screen.
