## Plan toward the date, not just the deadline

A wedding countdown is more than a number of days. It is a reminder of how much is left to plan, what is coming next, and how close the celebration is getting. Countdowns gives you one place to keep the main date visible while also tracking the smaller dates that lead up to it.

Use a dedicated list for wedding planning, choose a color that fits the event, and add the countdown to the screens you check most. When the date gets close, you can adjust units so the countdown becomes more precise without rebuilding it.

## Useful for couples, families, and guests

Countdowns works well whether you are planning the event, traveling to it, or simply waiting for the day to arrive. Add a widget for the ceremony date, create reminders for travel and packing, and keep past countdowns in the archive after the event has passed.
