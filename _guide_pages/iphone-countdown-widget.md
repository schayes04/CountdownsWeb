## Widgets make countdowns easier to notice

An event countdown is most useful when it is visible. With an iPhone countdown widget, you can see the remaining time without opening the app or searching through a calendar.

Use widgets for the dates that are fun to watch and the dates you do not want to forget. A birthday, flight, wedding, race, school deadline, launch, or holiday can all have its own place on the Home Screen.

## Good for favorite countdowns and lists

If you track several events, organize them into lists and choose which countdowns deserve Home Screen space. Keep the most important date visible and use the app for the full set.
