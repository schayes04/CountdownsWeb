## Track the vacation details that build anticipation

Theme park trips usually include more than one date. You may want to track the first travel day, hotel check-in, park reservations, dining plans, ticket windows, packing deadlines, and the day you come home. Countdowns keeps those dates together in a dedicated list.

The main countdown can be fun for the whole family, while reminders help the planner stay ahead of the less exciting details.

## One list for the whole trip

Create a theme park trip list and add each date as its own countdown. Use custom colors or icons to separate travel, parks, food, and planning tasks.
