## Make birthdays visible all year

Birthday countdowns are most useful when they do not need to be rebuilt every time they come back. With recurring events, you can create the birthday once and keep it ready for future celebrations. Add a reminder a few days or weeks ahead so planning starts before the calendar notification.

Widgets make the countdown easier to notice. Pin a child’s birthday, a partner’s birthday, or a list of upcoming birthdays where you already look during the day.

## Organize the people you celebrate

Instead of mixing every birthday into one long list, create groups that match your life. Family, friends, work, school, and kids can each have their own list, making it easier to see what is coming next.
