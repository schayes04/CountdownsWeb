## Count down to the date everyone is waiting for

A pregnancy countdown can make the due date feel more concrete while still leaving room for the many dates that happen before it. Countdowns can track the main due date and also hold appointments, classes, preparation tasks, or family travel plans.

Because every pregnancy timeline is personal, the app lets you choose your own labels, units, reminders, and organization.

## Flexible for practical planning

Use one list for pregnancy-related dates, then archive countdowns after they pass. You can keep the main due date visible with a widget while using reminders for practical preparation dates.
